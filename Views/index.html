Welcome to Tenteru!

Stay ahead in the tech world with Tenteru. Get personalized updates on tech events and conferences happening near you.

Here's how Tenteru works:
  • Customized Event Search: Tenteru helps you find tech events and conferences tailored to your location and preferences.
  • Stay Updated: Receive personalized event updates, ensuring you never miss out on opportunities to learn and network.
  • Effortless Process: Enter your location, preferred event type (e.g. tech), and month, and Tenteru will handle the rest.
  • Scraping Power: Tenteru uses advanced web scraping to fetch the latest tech events from reliable sources.
  • Email Notifications: As soon as the event data is available, Tenteru will email you the details using puppeteer.

Experience the future of staying informed in the tech world. Welcome to Tenteru, your personalized tech event companion.
